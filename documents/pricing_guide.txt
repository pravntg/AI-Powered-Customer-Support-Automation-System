ABC Technologies - Pricing Guide

1. Subscription Plans
ABC Technologies offers three subscription plans tailored for business management:

- Basic Plan
  - Price: $19 per month (billed monthly)
  - Features: Up to 5 user seats, core business management tools, 10GB shared storage, and standard email support.
  - Best for: Small startups and individual professionals.

- Professional Plan
  - Price: $49 per month (billed monthly)
  - Features: Unlimited user seats, advanced analytics, custom integrations, 100GB storage, and priority 24/7 email & chat support.
  - Best for: Growing mid-sized businesses.

- Enterprise Plan
  - Price: Custom pricing (starts at $199 per month, billed annually)
  - Features: Dedicated account manager, 99.9% SLA guarantee, custom features development, unlimited storage, and phone support.
  - Best for: Large organizations and high-volume operations.

2. Annual Billing Discount
- Customers who choose annual billing for the Basic or Professional plan receive a 20% discount on the total price.
- Basic Plan Annual: $182.40 per year (equivalent to $15.20/month).
- Professional Plan Annual: $470.40 per year (equivalent to $39.20/month).

3. Add-on Pricing
- Extra 50GB storage: $5 per month.
- Dedicated IP address: $10 per month.
- Advanced Security Package: $15 per month.
