ABC Technologies - Company Policy Document

1. Refund Policy
- Refund requests are eligible only within 14 days of the initial purchase or billing date.
- Any request for a refund must be reviewed and approved by a human supervisor to ensure it matches billing criteria.
- Automatic refunds are strictly prohibited to prevent fraud and account errors.

2. Subscription Cancellation
- Customers can cancel their subscription at any time via the User Dashboard.
- Cancellations sent to support must be manually processed and require supervisor review before finalizing, ensuring no active usage or pending payments remain.
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. No partial refunds are given for unused mid-cycle time unless explicitly approved by a supervisor.

3. Account Closure
- Requesting to permanently close or delete an account requires supervisor approval.
- Before closure, the system must verify that all invoices are paid, there are no active subscriptions, and user identity is verified.
- The supervisor must sign off on the deletion of data in compliance with general data protection regulations.

4. Compensation Policy
- In cases of system downtime or service degradation, customer support may offer compensation.
- Standard support agents can authorize compensation (credits or discounts) up to $50.
- Any compensation request exceeding $50, or requests for direct financial refunds as compensation, must be escalated to a human supervisor for approval.

5. Escalation to Management
- If a customer is dissatisfied with standard support or explicitly requests to speak with management, the request must be escalated.
- All escalations to management require human supervisor validation and approval before routing to upper management.
